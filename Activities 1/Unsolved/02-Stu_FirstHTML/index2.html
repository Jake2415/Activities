French Toast Turkey Sando

Is is for Breakfast, Lunch, or Dinner?

by Bon Appetit

Ingredients
12 ounce sweet potato, sliced into 1/4 inch rounds
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
8 ounces brussel sprouts, trimmed and thinly sliced
To Taste kosher salt
3/4 cup Heinz® Mayonnaise
8 thick slices white pullman or country bread
2 cups Stove Top Turkey Stuffing
2 teaspoons apple cider vinegar
2 cups red and/or white cabbage, finely shredded
1 carrot peeled and sliced into thin matchsticks
12 slices turkey breast, 1/8-in thin
3/4 cup cranberry sauce

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 425°. Toss sweet potatoes with 1 tablespoon oil. Toss brussels sprouts with 1 tablespoon oil. Arrange each on one half of rimmed baking sheet; season with salt and pepper. Roast until golden, 25 minutes.
  2. Heat large skillet over medium. Spread mayonnaise on both sides of bread, 1 tablespoon per slice. Working in batches, toast until golden, 1 to 2 minutes per side.
  3. Increase heat to medium-high. Add remaining oil and stuffing. Cook, turning occasionally, until crisp, 5 minutes.
  4. Whisk 1 tablespoon oil with vinegar. Toss with cabbage and carrot; season with salt and pepper.
  5. Assemble sandwich: Spread remaining mayonnaise on one side of each slice toasted bread. Top 4 slices with slaw, turkey, brussels sprouts, sweet potatoes, cranberry sauce, and stuffing. Close sandwiches.
